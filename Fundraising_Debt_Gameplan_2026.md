## Executive Summary

Multi-tranche financing roadmap across 5 containers. Container 1 is the key: once live and cash-generative (€4.7M FCF/year), it self-funds subsequent containers. First job is to get Container 1 fully capitalized and operational.

This gameplan assumes Container 1 financing is **not yet closed**. If it is, timeline shifts accordingly.

- **What:** Early relationship-building with infrastructure finance teams
- **Who:** Peter Damm
- **When:** Starts once Containers 1 and 2 are operating (Q4 2026)
- **Why:** Commercial banks want 2–3 containers of operating history before debt conversation. By then, you have it.
- **Deliverable:** Relationship established, preliminary mandate discussion

## Cadence Breach Protocol

If a scheduled meeting or ritual is missed:
1. Karen flags in Slack within 24h
2. Flags include: what was missed, who was responsible, proposed reschedule
3. Karen does not reschedule autonomously — proposes and waits for confirmation
4. Repeated breaches are noted in the Friday Ops Pulse

---

## Action Item Rules

Every action item requires: **owner · deadline · workstream**

- **Overdue:** deadline passed, no confirmed completion. Karen flags in Slack.
- **Blocker:** stated dependency unresolved more than 48h. Karen flags immediately.
- **Stale:** no update in 72h on an active item. Karen asks directly.
- **Resolved:** owner confirms done. Logged in action log. Marked closed.

"We'll look into it" is not an action item. Karen will ask for a name and a date.
